## 1. Overview

Akashic Resonance ("the App," "we," "our") is a consciousness-mapping and collective-resonance platform. Your privacy is foundational to our design. This policy describes what information we request, why we request it, how it is handled, and your rights regarding that information.

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

---

## 2. Information We Collect and How We Use It

### 2.1 Account Information (required)

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Email address | Account creation, authentication, and security notices. Never displayed publicly. |
| Alias / display name | Your chosen public identity within the collective field. |

### 2.2 Birth Data (transient — used once, then deleted)

During the first-time setup ("First Pulse" generation) you may provide:

- Birth date
- Approximate birth location (city or region — **no precise GPS coordinates**)
- Time of day (optional)

**This data is used solely to generate your Atomic Resonance ID and your First Pulse chart pack.**

Processing occurs on our secure servers (Base44 platform infrastructure). **Your raw birth data is permanently and irrevocably deleted from our systems immediately after your First Pulse chart is created.** A permanent audit flag (`birth_data_deleted = true`) is set in your record to confirm deletion. Only the generated chart output and a non-identifying location label (city/region) are retained.

### 2.3 App-Generated Identifiers

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Atomic Resonance ID** (`atomic_consciousness_number`) | A unique, system-generated anonymous identifier derived from your birth data. This ID — not your name or email — represents you in all collective-field data. |
| **Torus Domain** (`TOP` or `BOTTOM`) | System-assigned field role; not personally identifiable. |

### 2.4 Content You Create

Resonance records, pulse check-ins, journal entries, soundscapes, intention circles, and binaural studio sessions you create are stored under your Atomic Resonance ID.

### 2.5 Session Activity

Frequency channel selections, mood settings, and binaural configuration are stored under your Atomic Resonance ID only — never linked to your email or real name in any public or shared view.

## 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- Real name (beyond the alias you choose)
- Precise GPS or location coordinates
- Raw birth data after First Pulse generation
- Device advertising identifiers (IDFA / GAID) for advertising purposes
- Behavioral data for advertising or ad-targeting
- Biometric data
- Health or medical records

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose.

Your data may be processed by the following service providers solely to operate the App:

| Provider | Role | Data involved |
|----------|------|---------------|
| **Base44** | Backend infrastructure, authentication, secure data storage | Account email, Atomic ID, content you create |

All data transmitted to our servers travels over HTTPS (TLS). We do not integrate third-party advertising networks, behavioral analytics SDKs, or crash-reporting services that collect personal information.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of users, but only to the minimum extent required.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

| Data | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Birth data | **Deleted immediately** after First Pulse generation. |
| Account email | Retained while your account is active. Fully removed upon account deletion. |
| First Pulse chart output | Retained while your account is active. Deleted upon account deletion. |
| Resonance records and content | Retained while your account is active. Anonymized records may remain in aggregate collective-field data under your Atomic ID after account deletion unless you request full erasure. |

**To delete your account:** use the Account settings within the App. Your email will be fully removed from all records.

**To request complete data erasure** (including anonymized collective-field records), contact us at the email above.

## 6. Security

- All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption).
- Authentication is handled by Base44's secure platform infrastructure with role-restricted administrative access.
- Birth data is processed and deleted server-side; it is not cached or logged beyond the generation step.
- The platform undergoes periodic security review.

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Akashic Resonance is **not directed at children under the age of 13** (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that a child under the applicable age has created an account, we will delete that account and all associated data promptly.

If you believe a child has registered, please contact us at the email above.
